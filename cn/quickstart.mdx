## Setting Up Your Scarlet Device

This guide will walk you through the process of setting up your Scarlet device and connecting it to your gaming system.

### Hardware Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="microchip" title="Unbox your Scarlet device">
    Your Scarlet package should include:
    - Scarlet DMA device
    - USB-C cable
    - Quick start guide
    - Optional: PCIe adapter (depending on your model)
    
    Carefully remove all components from the packaging.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="plug" title="Connect your device">
    1. Connect the USB-C cable to your Scarlet device
    2. Connect the other end to your PC or gaming console
    3. If using PCIe, install the adapter according to the included instructions
    4. Power on your Scarlet device by pressing the power button
    
    The LED indicator should light up, showing that the device is powered on.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Software Installation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="download" title="Download Scarlet Software">
    Visit our [download page](https://scarlet.technology/download) to get the latest version of the Scarlet software for your operating system:
    - Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
    - macOS (Intel/Apple Silicon)
    - Linux (Ubuntu/Debian)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="gear" title="Install and configure">
    1. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions
    2. Launch the Scarlet application
    3. When prompted, connect your Scarlet device
    4. The software will automatically detect your device and install any necessary drivers
    5. Follow the on-screen setup wizard to complete the configuration
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## First-Time Setup

### Device Registration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    If you don't already have a Scarlet account, you'll need to create one. Click on "Register" in the Scarlet application and follow the instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your device">
    Enter your device's serial number (found on the bottom of the device or on the packaging) to register it to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your subscription">
    If you purchased a subscription, enter your activation code or log in with the account you used for purchase.
  </Step>
</Steps>
