# Before You Start
1. Uninstall all antivirus software on the computer, including Avast, Malwarebytes, 360, Master Lu, and others.  
2. Disable the built-in antivirus on the computer. (Windows Defender)
3. Use the `DX Repair Tool`.
4. Close and exit all overlay software, such as `GAME++`, `MSI Afterburner`, `NVIDIA GeForce`, `OneDrive` and etc..  
5. Close and exit any debuggers on your computer, like `CheatEngine`, `IDA`, `Scylla` and etc.
6. Uninstall any emulators on your computer, such as `Tencent Emulator`, `Gameloop Emulator`, `Thunder Emulator` and etc.
