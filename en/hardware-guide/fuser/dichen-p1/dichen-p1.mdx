# Dichen P1 4K@165Hz Fuser

## Product Overview

<Note>
The Dichen P1 is our premium fuser solution, supporting up to 4K resolution at 165Hz refresh rate.
</Note>

## Specifications

<CardGroup>
  <Card title="Display Output" icon="display">

    - Refresh Rate: Up to 165Hz
    - HDR Support: Yes
