<Warning>The account for Loader is separate from the account on the website.</Warning>
<Warning>All operations are performed on the second computer. Your gaming computer does not require any operations or downloads.</Warning>
<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Loader & Place it in a folder">
    Download Loader from card above. After download, please place it into an empty folder before run it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register an account on Loader & Login once">
    <Note>
    Username & Password length must `more than 3` and `less than 10`
    </Note>
    <Tip>When running the Loader for the first time, it is normal for an error to appear in the console.</Tip>
    1. Run `Scarlet.exe` as **Administrator**.
    2. Goto `Register` Tab and Register your account.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/en/images/InjectCard0.png" />
    </Frame>
    3. After registering, goto `Login` and login the account.  
    (Before recharge subscription, you should log into the account once to download all dependency files before recharging the account.)
    <Frame caption="Some Error appeared is common for first time">
      <img src="/en/images/InjectCard1.png" />
    </Frame>
    4. It make take a while to download all the files. Once it complete, the available products tab will be appeared.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/en/images/InjectCard3.png" />
    </Frame>
    5. After the products rab appeared, you can now close it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recharge Account's Subscription">
    1. Run again `Scarlet.exe` as **ADMINISTRATOR**.
    2. Goto `Recharge` Tab, fill in your registered username, password and card (license key).
    3. Click `Recharge`.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/en/images/InjectCard4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login and Load the software">
    <Tip>Only one software can access the DMA at a time; Yyou cannot run multiple DMA software or run DMA software alongside with DMA Speed Test software simultaneously.</Tip>
    1. After recharged successful, goto `Login` tab and login.
    2. The expiration time of the recharged game subscription should now be displayed in green. Click `Start`.
    3. Once the menu pops up, you can enjoy it!
  </Step>
</Steps>
